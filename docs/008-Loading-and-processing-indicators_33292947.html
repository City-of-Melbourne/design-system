Digital Customer Interface : 008 Loading and processing indicators

Created by Richard Collinson, last modified on Aug 28, 2018

What is it?

A visual indicator displayed while the customer waits for data to send, be retrieved or attached. 
When a customer sends a report, or attaches a file, we display an indication of progress to assure the customer something is happening while they wait for the form to complete the task. 
If we don't have these, then the experience could be negatively affected as the form may appear to have frozen, when in fact it's actually compressing a file or sending data to Salesforce. 
This is more important for customers using lower connection speeds (for example, 3G mobile)
